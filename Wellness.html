What Is Gluten & Can It Be Part of a "Healthy" Diet?


Gluten is a protein found in grains like wheat, barley, and rye. For most people, gluten is a safe and beneficial part of a balanced diet, as grains provide essential nutrients and energy. However, some individuals with celiac disease, gluten sensitivity, or a wheat allergy must avoid gluten, as it can trigger adverse reactions.

Social Media & Mental Health.



Social media can have both positive and negative effects on mental health, with potential benefits like enhanced connection and increased self-esteem, but also risks like anxiety, depression, and loneliness. Excessive use and exposure to certain content can negatively impact mental wellbeing, especially in young people.

Recovering Quickly From Winter Illness.

Stay Hydrated

Staying hydrated is important all year round, but even more so in the winter when the air is dry and cold and we spend a lot of time indoors breathing air with less ventilation and air conditioning.

Warm fluids such as broths, teas, soups and herbal tonics help to keep hydrated and provide vitamins, minerals and antioxidants to provide you with the building blocks you need to support your body's natural healing abilities.

Building Healthy Relationships.

Building healthy relationships involves mutual respect, open communication, trust, and understanding. It requires effort from both parties to create a safe space where each person can feel valued and heard. Healthy relationships also involve setting and respecting boundaries, and resolving conflicts constructively.

Career Wellness and life balance.

Career wellness and work-life balance are intertwined concepts focusing on achieving a healthy equilibrium between professional responsibilities and personal life. Career wellness emphasizes maintaining physical, mental, and emotional well-being while pursuing a career, while work-life balance aims to manage the time and energy allocated to both work and personal commitment.
